Artificial Intelligence Intern
DAR AL-HANDASAH (SHAIR & PARTNERS) — IT Department, AI Track

Building and evaluating a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline over the CIS Controls v8 document, from raw PDF to a measured, benchmarked answer.

Parse→ Chunk→ Embed→ Store→ Retrieve→ Generate→ Evaluate
  • Parsed source documents with unstructured (hi-res strategy) and chunked with a recursive character splitter.
  • Embedded with BAAI/bge-small-en-v1.5 and stored vectors in Weaviate for retrieval.
  • Added cross-encoder reranking (ms-marco-MiniLM-L6-v2) to sharpen retrieved results before generation.
  • Compared generation quality between the Gemini API and local Ollama (llama3.2:3b).
  • Evaluated retrieval against a hand-labeled golden set — Precision, Recall, F1, and Hit Rate at k=1 and k=3 — and extended evaluation with DeepEval metrics.
